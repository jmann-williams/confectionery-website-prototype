About Me

Coco M. Jones

My name is Coco M. Jones, and I started selling homemade chocolates in downtown Ottawa, Canada in 2007. After 10+ years of crafting fine chocolates, you get to know a thing or two about doing it right. Everything I make at Coco’s Chocolatiers is hand-crafted with 100% sustainable cocoa. We source premium-quality ingredients from around the world, and choose local options whenever possible. I never compromise on quality, and you bet you can taste it. Coco’s Chocolatiers is a family-owned company that's proudly all about chocolate.
